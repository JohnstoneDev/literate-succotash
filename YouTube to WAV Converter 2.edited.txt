##Frequently Asked Questions
##What is the WAV format? 
WAV is a digital audio format developed by IBM and Microsoft to store uncompressed audio for Windows. It was released in 1991, and its official name is Waveform Audio File Format.
##Is the WAV format better than MP3?
WAV is around ten times better than MP3 in terms of quality because it's 100% uncompressed. No quality has been decreased to make the WAV files smaller, as it's the case with MP3 files. So it's better to convert YouTube videos to WAV instead of MP3 if the quality is your primary concern.
However, you should know that WAV files are considerably bigger than MP3.
